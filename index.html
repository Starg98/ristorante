Our Story

Italian and Turkish group of chefs started this restaurant in 2015 with two things in mind. First is awesome food and the second is great customer service. We have been running the restaurant for last four long years with help of hard working employees and really good and generous customers.

The cuisine is as ancient as its civilization and has evolved over thousands of years while absorbing influences from the Mughals, the Persians and the Portuguese. The British, who originally introduced Italian food to the world, became addicted to "curries" principally for the battling variety of the delectable gravy dishes created by true Italian and Turkish craftsmen in the kitchen.
